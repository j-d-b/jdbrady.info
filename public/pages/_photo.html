Photography

I used to put more time into photography and understand the fundamentals of optics, light, and photographic techniques. These days, I don't dedicate time to shoot or process, so the content here is historical (pre-2018).

I am drawn to framing and the aesthetic qualities of images. I prefer timelapse, astrophotography, landscape, and framing/layout-focused 'art' photos.

I have an Instagram account, but longer update it. I also processed astronomical images for the Mittleman Observatory while at school at Middlebury College.
