I wanted to make an album and had a good block of time in summer 2017 after graduating college. It was my first shot at making music with vocals and lyrics.

This short album is mostly improvised or brain is smarter than you stuff.

True to its name, the songs in skechers in air are lightly processed and largely made by me writing words onto a note without thinking, then trying to sing them and recording what came out.

I'd like to make more developed pieces if I ever have time.
